Welcome to Congestion Control System

Overview

The Congestion Control System is a robust solution designed to manage and optimize your network infrastructure. It ensures efficient utilization of resources, minimizes congestion, and provides real-time insights into your network's performance.

Network Devices

Easily add, update, and delete network devices. Each device comes with detailed information, including its name, status, and network rate. Keep your inventory organized and up-to-date to make informed decisions about your network infrastructure.

System Dashboard

Monitor the health and performance of your network in real-time. The system dashboard provides comprehensive visualizations of CPU usage, memory usage, and other vital metrics. Stay informed and proactive in managing your network resources.

Key Benefits

  • Optimize network performance and prevent congestion.
  • Efficiently manage and monitor all network devices from a centralized platform.
  • Real-time insights enable quick response to potential issues.
  • Improve resource utilization and reduce downtime.
  • User-friendly interface for easy navigation and configuration.
